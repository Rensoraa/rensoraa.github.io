Rensora - Galaxy Life

Galaxy Life

Short version - About This Game:
Galaxy Life is a space-themed strategy game where players build and manage their own intergalactic empire. Players can explore the galaxy, form alliances, and engage in battles with other players to expand their territory and resources.

Long Version - About This Game:

Impressive planets, strange creatures, and constant danger await in Galaxy Life! In this online game, you guide your colony to victory. Your cute little Starlings may look harmless, but space is full of threats.
Explore the Galaxy Life universe, build your colony, attack enemies, collect items, and aim to rule the galaxy. Discover unique planets and characters in this free-to-play MMO strategy game that blends casual mechanics with tower defense, RTS elements, and competitive PvP in a persistent online galaxy. Play with others or team up with friends to fight evil in this social space adventure!

Features:

Persistent galaxy at war with players worldwide
Battle friends or foes for loot and galactic control
Protect your colonies with towers, mines, bunkers, walls, shields, traps, and more
Conquer weird and intriguing new worlds
Form alliances and wage epic Alliance Wars
Bet and fight others in Rival Battles
Climb weekly, monthly, or all-time leaderboards
Seasonal events like Halloween or Doomsday
Frequent updates with new features, content, and fixes
Train and upgrade troops and resource buildings
Craft Special Attacks and unique units with collectables
Cooperate with friends through multiple support options
Save your Starlings from the evil Firebit
